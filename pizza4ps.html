[NEW WASTE MANAGEMENT PROGRAM] BRING PLASTIC WASTE, GET REWARDS FROM BKK!

Details

Nature has given us the environment, and it is our job to protect it from harmful effects since we have no other comfort zone.
Plastic waste is one of the elements contributing to the decline of nature’s health and must be managed properly.

As a restaurant with the goal of being zero waste, Pizza 4P’s is glad and excited to introduce a new program in which you will earn points on a stamp card for bringing plastic garbage of any kind to our Recycle Hub at BKK. We jointly call for collective action against indiscriminate disposal of plastic and all other recyclable wastes.

Bring a minimum of 300g of plastic waste to our Hub, and you’ll instantly receive a point stamp that can be exchanged for rewards:
– Free drink for 1 stamp
– Free coaster for 5 stamps
– Free pizza for 10 stamps

Our objective is to advocate for sustainable solutions to all forms of environmental degradation and reduce the negative effect of climate change. Join us today!
